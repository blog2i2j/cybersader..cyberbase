# About ConfessIt

ConfessIt is a Roman Catholic examination of conscience designed for computers, tablets, and phones. It’s simple and easy to use, aiding in remembering sins for confession. The app includes a confession walkthrough, detailing priest responses and suggested answers – beneficial for infrequent confessions. The examination of conscience is rooted in fundamental Catholic teachings, is accessible, and relevant for modern Catholics.

## Key Features

*   Catholic Examination of Conscience
*   Simple and Easy to Use
*   Confession Walkthrough (priest responses & suggested answers)
*   Based on Fundamental Catholic Teachings
*   Accessible for Modern Catholics
