# hledger Overview

 hledger is a plain text accounting tool designed for ease of use. It provides a flexible and customizable way to track finances. The site offers resources for getting started, installation, and understanding the tool's features. It includes documentation, a cookbook, and support resources.

## Getting Started

 The site provides guides for installation, getting started, and accessing the manual. It also includes a 'Cookbook' for common journal entries and workflows.

## Features

 hledger supports features such as: account management, budgeting, forecasting, return on investment (ROI) calculation, gain/loss calculation, currency conversion, and error checking. It also offers support for common journal entries and workflows.

## Documentation & Support

 The site offers a comprehensive manual, FAQs, videos/talks, and a support/discussion forum. It also includes developer documentation and resources.

## Finance & Sponsorship

 The project finances are transparently managed, and sponsorship is available to support the development of hledger. Regression bounties are offered to incentivize bug fixes.

## All Pages

The site covers a wide range of accounting topics, including: accounting basics, budgeting and forecasting, the accounting equation, loans and lending, and foreign trip expenses. It also includes charts and graphs for visualizing financial data.
