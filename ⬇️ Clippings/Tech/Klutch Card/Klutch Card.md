# Klutch Card Summary

Klutch Card is an app-powered credit card offering users complete control over their spending and financial management. The card provides a suite of features accessible through a dedicated mobile application, designed to cater to diverse financial needs and preferences. Key features include:

*   **Mini Apps:** A collection of specialized apps designed for specific use cases, such as:
    *   **Klutch AI Insights:** Provides AI-driven insights into spending habits.
    *   **Single-Use Card:** Allows for temporary credit limits.
    *   **Merchant Card:** Facilitates business spending.
    *   **Subscription Monitor:** Tracks and manages recurring subscriptions.
    *   **Child Allowances:** Enables setting and managing allowances for children.
    *   **If / Then:** Creates custom spending rules and alerts.
    *   **Custom Autopay:** Automates bill payments.
    *   **Google Sheet Sync:** Integrates with Google Sheets for financial tracking.
    *   **Climate Impact:** Tracks and potentially offsets carbon emissions from purchases.
    *   **Advanced Controls:** Provides granular control over spending limits and categories.
    *   **Spending Categories:** Allows users to categorize their spending for better analysis.
    *   **Monthly Budget:** Helps users create and stick to a monthly budget.
    *   **Klutch Deals:** Offers exclusive deals and discounts.
    *   **FSA Auto Claimer:** Automates the process of claiming FSA funds.

*   **User Control:** The core value proposition is centered around user control, allowing individuals to set rules, manage spending, and track their finances.

*   **Integration:** The card integrates with Google Sheets for enhanced financial tracking.

*   **Partnerships:** Klutch Card is open to partnerships.

*   **Sign-Up:** Users can sign up for a Klutch Card through the app or website.

*   **FAQs:** Frequently asked questions are available on the website.

*   **Support:** Customer support is available via email and contact form.

*   **Login:** Users can log in to the Klutch Card app.
