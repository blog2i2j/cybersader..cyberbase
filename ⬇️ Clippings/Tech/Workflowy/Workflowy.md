# Workflowy - A Simplified Organization Tool

Workflowy is a tool designed for individuals with demanding jobs or ambitious projects, offering a streamlined approach to staying organized. It focuses on collecting ideas, developing them, sharing them, and transforming them into tangible realities.

## Key Features & Benefits

*   **Idea Capture:** Facilitates the collection of ideas.
*   **Development:** Supports the expansion and refinement of ideas.
*   **Sharing:** Enables the dissemination of ideas.
*   **Realization:** Assists in converting ideas into actionable outcomes.
