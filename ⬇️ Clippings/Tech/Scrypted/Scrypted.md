# Scrypted - Video Integration Platform

Scrypted is a video integration platform designed for seamless streaming and recording.

## Overview

Scrypted offers a comprehensive solution for integrating video surveillance systems with various smart home and streaming platforms.

## Key Features

*   **Video Streaming:** Supports streaming to a range of devices including HomeKit, Google Home, Nest Hub, Chromecast, Android TV, and the Google Home app.
*   **Scrypted NVR:** A plugin providing 24/7 recording and smart detections, with mobile and desktop applications.
*   **HomeKit Integration:** Enables streaming to HomeKit, including HomeKit Secure Video recorded events in iCloud and smart detections.
*   **Google Home Integration:** Supports streaming to Nest Hub, Chromecast, Android TV, and the Google Home Android app.
*   **Alexa Integration:** Facilitates streaming to Echo devices and the Alexa app on iOS and Android.
*   **Home Assistant Integration:** Seamless integration with Lovelace, including custom low latency cards.
