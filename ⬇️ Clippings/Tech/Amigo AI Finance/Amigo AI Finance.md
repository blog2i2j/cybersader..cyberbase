> [amigo.finance > Amigo AI - Expense Manager](https://www.amigo.finance/)

Amigo AI - Expense Manager is an AI-powered personal finance tool that leverages language models to provide personalized financial insights. It analyzes users' transaction data, emails, and statements to offer tailored recommendations and smart budgeting tools. The platform includes a home page, pricing information, calculators, promo codes, and an 'about us' section. It's available on iOS and Android, with over 1,000 happy users. The company was initially supported by a startup program and has received support from Google and Mixpanel. The platform's core functionality centers around AI-driven expense management and financial intelligence.
