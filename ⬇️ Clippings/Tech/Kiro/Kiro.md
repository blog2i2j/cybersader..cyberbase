## Kiro: Your AI Coach for 24/7 Personalized Financial Guidance

Kiro is an AI-powered financial coaching solution designed for Gen Z and Millennials, developed by financial experts. It transforms complexity into clarity, offering personalized advice and tangible steps to achieve financial goals. 

**For Institutions:**
*   Boost engagement with a simple conversational chatbot.
*   Strengthen loyalty through co-branded experiences.
*   Gain valuable insights into member needs and behavior.

**For Users:**
*   24/7 money sidekick, here to answer all your financial questions—no judgment, no stress, no side-eyes. Whether it’s saving, budgeting, investing, or taxes, getting answers is as easy as texting your finance-savvy friend.

Kiro is trusted by financial institutions like Aviva, Forbes, and NatWest, and is built on a foundation of expertise and designed for the next generation.  It provides a user-friendly experience for financial guidance.
