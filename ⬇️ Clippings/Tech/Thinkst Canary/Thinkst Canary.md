# Thinkst Canary

Thinkst Canary is a honeypot and deception technology solution designed to detect attackers and breaches proactively. It offers rapid deployment (2 minutes), minimal false positives, and no ongoing overhead. Canaries are available in Hardware, VM, and Cloud-based configurations and are deployed globally across all 7 continents. Key features include:

- **Rapid Deployment:** Setup in just 2 minutes.
- **Low False Positives:** Nearly 0 false positives, reducing alert fatigue.
- **No Ongoing Overhead:** Minimal maintenance and management requirements.
- **Global Coverage:** Available in Hardware, VM, and Cloud-based Canaries deployed worldwide.
