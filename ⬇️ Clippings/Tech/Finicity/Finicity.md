# Finicity Open Banking Solutions

Finicity is part of the Mastercard family, providing an open banking platform that delivers financial data. The platform focuses on consumer-permissioned data, empowering innovation and unlocking possibilities for customers and their businesses. 

## Solutions

Finicity offers two primary solutions:

### Lend

*   **Description:** FCRA-compliant data for lending decisions and verification.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   Assets
    *   Income
    *   Employment
    *   Cash Flow
    *   SMB Lending
    *   Statements
    *   Transactions
    *   Scoring Attributes

### Manage

*   **Description:** Cleaned, categorized, and aggregated account data.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   Account History
    *   Statements
