# Buckets Private Family Budgeting App

Buckets is a private family budgeting application designed for simplicity and control over personal finances. It offers a free trial with no monthly fees, costing a one-time purchase of $64. The application is available for macOS, Windows, Linux, iOS, and Android.

**Key Features:**

*   **Private Finances:** Data remains on the user's computer, providing control over financial information.
*   **No Monthly Fees:** A single purchase grants access for all users within the household.
*   **Cross-Platform Compatibility:** Available on macOS, Windows, Linux, iOS, and Android.
*   **Envelope Budgeting:** Supports a form of envelope budgeting or customizable methods.
*   **Bank Synchronization:** Includes novel methods for syncing with banks, utilizing SimpleFIN Bridge or manual macros/file imports.
*   **Support & Resources:** Offers helpful videos and a chat bubble for support. Includes a changelog, guide, and blog.

**Download Options:**

The application can be downloaded directly from the website or through the respective app stores.  The download links are provided for each platform.

**Testimonials:**

User feedback highlights the application's simplicity, effectiveness, and ability to help users achieve financial goals, such as owning a home.

**Additional Information:**
The application includes fast Amazon reconciliation and is designed to help users manage their finances effectively and privately.
