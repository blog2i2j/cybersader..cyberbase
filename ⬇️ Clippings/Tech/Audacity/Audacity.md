# Audacity: Free Audio Editor and Recorder

Audacity is the world’s most popular audio editing and recording app. It offers powerful features for editing, mixing, and enhancing audio tracks. Downloadable via MuseHub or directly.

## Key Features

*   **Cross-Platform Compatibility:** Runs on Windows, macOS, and Linux.
*   **Audio Format Support:** Imports and exports all major audio formats (WAV, MP3, FLAC, Ogg, etc.).
*   **Plugin Support:** Extensive support for third-party plugins (VST3, Nyquist).
*   **Deep Audio Analysis:** Spectrogram view and Vamp analyzers for detailed audio analysis.
*   **Collaboration:** Audio.com companion for collaborative projects, versioned backups, and easy sharing.
