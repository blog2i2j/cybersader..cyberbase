# Castopod: Free & Open-Source Podcast Hosting

Castopod is a free and open-source podcast hosting platform designed for podcasters who want to maintain control over their content and engage directly with their audience. The platform emphasizes self-hosting and eliminates intermediaries.

## Key Features

*   **Turnkey Offers:** Launch your own Castopod instance in just 5 minutes using their turnkey offers.
*   **Self-Hosting:**  Easily self-host your podcasts, retaining full control.
*   **Direct Audience Interaction:** Communicate with your audience without relying on third-party platforms.
*   **Open Source:** Benefit from the transparency and community-driven development of an open-source solution.
*   **Documentation & Support:** Comprehensive documentation and a supportive community.
*   **Source Code Availability:** Access the source code for customization and deeper understanding.
*   **Fediverse Integration:** Follow Castopod on the Fediverse for updates and community engagement.
*   **Downloadable Installer:**  A downloadable installer (Castopod-1.13.4.zip) for easy setup.
