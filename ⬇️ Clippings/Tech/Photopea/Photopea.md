# Photopea Online Photo Editor

**Overview:** Photopea is a free online photo editor offering professional-grade tools without downloads or installations. It's fully local, cost-effective, and convenient, running on any device.

**Key Features:**

*   **Fully Local Operation:** Runs on your device, utilizing CPU and GPU, ensuring instant file access and privacy.
*   **Cost-Effective:** Provides premium features without charge.
*   **Convenient Editor:** Accessible through any browser, eliminating software installation.
*   **Professional Editor:** Supports a comprehensive suite of tools, including layers, masks, blending, and PSD support.
*   **Format Support:** Handles over 40 file formats, including PSD, PNG, JPG, GIF, BMP, WEBP, SVG, PDF, AI, AVIF, DDS, HEIC, TIFF, MP4, TGA, CDR, PDN, EPS, INDD, Figma, and more.
*   **RAW Support:** Opens DNG, CR2, CR3, NEF, ARW, RW2, RAF, ORF, and FFF files with exposure and color balance adjustments.
*   **AI-Powered Features:** One-click background removal and image replacement via text description.
*   **Vector Graphics:** Supports creation and editing of vector graphics.

**Target Audience:** Designers, photographers, and anyone needing a powerful, free photo editing tool.
