> [venmo.com > Pay Friends | Payments App | Venmo](https://venmo.com/)

Venmo is a mobile payments app that allows users to send and receive money from friends and family. It offers features like bill splitting, payments, and social payments. The app is designed for easy and secure transactions within a network of contacts. It's a popular choice for splitting expenses and facilitating peer-to-peer payments. The app is free to use and available on both iOS and Android devices. It's built on a social payment platform, enabling users to connect and transact with their network. The app's core functionality revolves around facilitating simple and secure money transfers.
