> [actions.work > Actions For Obsidian](https://actions.work/actions-for-obsidian/)

Actions For Obsidian is a macOS/iOS app offering 50+ shortcuts to integrate Obsidian with Apple's automation ecosystem. It provides deep integration with macOS/iOS and Apple's automation ecosystem. It offers a 14-day trial with a one-time payment. The app is designed to bridge the gap between Obsidian and automation workflows. The app is available on the App Store and offers documentation and a forum for support.
