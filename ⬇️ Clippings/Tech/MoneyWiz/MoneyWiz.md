# MoneyWiz 2025 - Personal Accounting

## Overview
MoneyWiz 2025 is a personal accounting software designed to provide users with money management superpowers. It combines the power of accounting software with the ease of personal finance software.

## Key Features
*   **Bank Sync:** Connects to over 40,000 banks in 50+ countries via 4 data providers, automatically categorizing transactions, detecting transfers & refunds, and reconciling accounts.
*   **Cloud Sync:** Works offline and syncs automatically when online, backed by 256-bit military-grade encryption for data transfer and storage.
*   **Fast Updates:** Releases new features almost weekly, with a transparent roadmap for future development.
*   **Cross-Platform:** Fully featured on desktop & mobile (iOS & macOS), with Apple Wallet support.
