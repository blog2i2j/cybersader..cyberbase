> [copilot.money > Do money better with Copilot](https://copilot.money/)

Copilot Money is a Mac, iPhone, and iPad app designed to help users manage their finances effectively. It tracks spending, investments, and net worth, providing a comprehensive view of personal finances. The app is available on the Apple App Store and has received overwhelmingly positive reviews (4.858,500+ reviews) including coverage from publications like The New York Times. Key features include tracking spending, budgeting, investment monitoring, and net worth calculation. The app is presented with various static images and a video showcasing its functionality. The app's core value proposition is to simplify financial management and empower users to 'do money better'.
