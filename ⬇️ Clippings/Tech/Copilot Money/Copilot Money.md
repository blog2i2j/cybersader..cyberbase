## Copilot Money: Overview

Copilot Money is a best-in-class app available for Mac, iPhone, and iPad designed to help users manage their finances effectively. It allows users to track their spending, investments, and net worth in one place.

## Key Features

*   **Spending Tracking:** Monitor your expenses to understand where your money is going.
*   **Budgeting:** Create and manage budgets to stay on track with your financial goals.
*   **Investment Tracking:** Track the performance of your investments.
*   **Net Worth Calculation:** Calculate your net worth to see your overall financial health.
