> [mybudgetcoach.com > MyBudgetCoach](https://www.mybudgetcoach.com/)

MyBudgetCoach is a zero-based budgeting app offering personalized coaching. Users connect their banks, categorize transactions, and work with a certified budget coach at their own pace. The app provides a 35-day free trial at $14.99/month after the trial period, with no credit card required. Key features include financial clarity, affordability, and a customized spending plan. The website offers resources for graduates and provides information on pricing, blog content, and coach matching. It also includes a quiz to start a free trial.
