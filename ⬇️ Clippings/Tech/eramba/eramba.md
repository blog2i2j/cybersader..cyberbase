## Eramba Free GRC Software Overview

Eramba is a free, open-source Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) solution developed by a community of CISOs. It's designed to be affordable and simple, eliminating the need for expensive, complex, or professional support-dependent GRC tools. The current version is 3.26.2, last updated 19 days ago.

## Key Features

*   Free and Open Source
*   Community-Driven
*   Simple and Affordable
*   No Professional Support Dependency

## Learning Portal

Eramba offers a learning portal with video training materials.  A key learning resource is the "Eramba Live Demo (Spanish)" available at [https://wkf.ms/42xWDVQ](https://wkf.ms/42xWDVQ).

## About Eramba

Eramba is built by a community of CISOs and has been used by thousands for over a decade. It's focused on avoiding complicated and expensive GRC tools. The team is allergic to complicated, expensive, professional support dependent GRC tools.
