# ProjectionLab Overview

ProjectionLab is a personal finance simulator developed by Kyle Nolan, launched in 2021. The platform's core goal is to provide users with a fun and data-respecting tool for visualizing their financial plans and scenarios.  It aims to help users understand their investments, expenses, and long-term financial goals.

## Key Features

*   **Data Respect:** Prioritizes user data privacy and security.
*   **Interactive Simulation:** Allows users to model various financial scenarios.
*   **Visualization:** Presents financial data in an easily understandable format.
*   **Scenario Planning:** Enables users to explore different investment strategies and life events.

## Development History

Since its inception in 2021, ProjectionLab has evolved significantly. The platform has gained traction and expanded its capabilities over the past two years.
