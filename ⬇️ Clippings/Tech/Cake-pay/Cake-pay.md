# Cake Pay Overview

Cake Pay is a cryptocurrency payment system developed by Cake Labs LLC, launched in 2018. It enables users to seamlessly spend their cryptocurrency on everyday purchases through a network of gift cards, Visa, and Mastercard. The system prioritizes user privacy by eliminating the need for traditional banking intermediaries and ID verification.

## Key Features

*   **Cryptocurrency Payments:** Allows spending of cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin, Ethereum, Monero, and others.
*   **Gift Card Integration:** Supports spending crypto on a wide range of gift cards from popular retailers.
*   **Visa & Mastercard Support:** Enables direct spending via Visa and Mastercard.
*   **Privacy Focused:** Eliminates the need for banks and ID verification.
*   **Low Fees:**  Cards cost exactly their value.
*   **No Custodian:** Users maintain full control of their funds.

## Retailer Partnerships

The platform currently supports payments at retailers including DoorDash, Amazon, Best Buy, eBay, Target, Walmart, and Uber.

## User Benefits

Cake Pay empowers users to live their lives without sacrificing privacy for convenience. It offers a secure and private way to spend cryptocurrency on everyday purchases, eliminating the need for cash and traditional banking systems.
