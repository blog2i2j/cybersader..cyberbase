# Emma Budget App Overview

Emma is a comprehensive budgeting and financial management app designed to help users take control of their finances. It offers features for tracking spending, managing subscriptions, budgeting, analyzing net worth, and even investing and exploring crypto. The app’s core functionality revolves around connecting all accounts to provide a holistic view of a user’s financial situation.

## Key Features

*   **Everyday Spending Tracking:** Automatically categorizes and tracks daily expenses.
*   **Account Aggregation:** Securely connects to bank accounts for seamless data integration.
*   **Recurring Payments Management:** Identifies and manages recurring bills and subscriptions.
*   **Budgeting:** Allows users to set budgets and track progress.
*   **Analytics:** Provides insights into spending patterns and financial health.
*   **Net Worth Tracking:** Calculates and monitors net worth over time.
*   **Savings Tools:** Facilitates saving goals and cashback rewards.
*   **Sharing & Groups:** Enables users to share spending data and collaborate within groups.
*   **Investment Options:** Offers access to stocks and cryptocurrency investments.
*   **Credit Boosting:** Provides tools for improving credit scores.
*   **Rent Reporting:** Simplifies rent payment tracking.

## Plans

Emma offers several subscription plans to cater to different user needs:

*   **Free:** Basic budgeting and expense tracking.
*   **Plus:** Includes exclusive cashback deals and bill reminders.
*   **Pro:** Provides advanced budgeting features, offline account access, and accurate net worth tracking.
*   **Ultimate:** Designed for business accounts and offers Spaces for managing multiple accounts.
