# Peek - AI-Powered Financial Wellness App

Peek is an AI-powered personal finance app designed to help users understand their spending habits and build better financial habits without shame or anxiety. It auto-tracks income and expenses, provides 'vibe checks' to assess spending patterns, and generates personalized to-do lists.

**Key Features & Functionality:**

*   **Automatic Expense Tracking:** Automatically categorizes and tracks spending.
*   **Vibe Checks:** Provides insights into spending habits and identifies potential problem areas.
*   **To-Do Lists:** Generates personalized to-do lists based on financial goals.
*   **Personalized Insights:** Offers tailored feedback and recommendations based on individual spending patterns.
*   **Emotional Support:** Designed to address the emotional aspects of money management, reducing anxiety and shame.

**Example Insights & Feedback:**

*   Identifies spending on Uber Eats and delivery fees as a potential issue.
*   Highlights the impact of weekend spending on overall finances.
*   Recognizes the tendency to spend on snacks while waiting for coffee.
*   Acknowledges the increase in net worth due to a single houseplant.
*   Recognizes the difference in saving habits between weekdays and weekends.

**User Feedback & Tone:**

The app's messaging emphasizes a supportive and understanding approach to money management, framing it as a conversation with a friend rather than a judgmental assessment.

**Overall Purpose:**

Peek aims to provide a user-friendly and emotionally intelligent tool for managing finances, promoting financial wellness and reducing the stress associated with money management.
