> [hushed.com > The Temporary & Second Phone Number App For Wifi Calling](https://hushed.com/)

Hushed is a second phone number app for Wi-Fi calling, offering private calls and text messaging. It allows users to manage multiple numbers on a single device. The app is available for Android and iOS. Key features include secure communication, multiple number management, and support for Wi-Fi calling. The app is ideal for scenarios such as long-term care communication and secure personal communication. The website provides information on pricing, features, a blog, and login details. The app can be downloaded from the App Store and Google Play.
