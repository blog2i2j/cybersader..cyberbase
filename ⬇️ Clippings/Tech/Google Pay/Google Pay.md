# Google Pay Overview

Google Pay is a quick, easy, and secure way to pay online, in stores, or send money to friends and family. It’s designed to be the ‘Google way’ of payment.

## Key Features

*   **Pay In Store:** Enables contactless payments in physical stores.
*   **Pay Online:** Facilitates secure online purchases.
*   **Pay Each Other:** Allows users to send and receive money directly with contacts.
*   **Google Wallet Integration:** Seamlessly connects with Google Wallet for comprehensive financial management.
*   **Safety Center:** Provides access to safety resources and controls.
*   **Business Solutions:** Offers features tailored for businesses.
