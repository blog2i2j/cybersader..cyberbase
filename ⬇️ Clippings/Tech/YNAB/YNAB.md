# YNAB - Financial Planning and Budgeting

This webpage promotes YNAB (You Need A Budget), a financial planning and budgeting app designed to help users control their spending and saving. The primary message is that users can work harder without seeing the results, and YNAB provides a solution for efficient money management.

## Key Features and Benefits

*   **Australian Focus:** The website immediately recognizes the user's location as Australia and directs them to the appropriate regional website.
*   **Free Trial Offer:** A significant incentive is presented – a free month of YNAB, with an additional free month offered when a user subscribes through a friend’s referral.
*   **YNAB Method:** YNAB emphasizes its core methodology, a set of habits designed for guilt-free spending.
*   **Educational Resources:** The page provides extensive resources to learn about YNAB, including:
    *   **What is YNAB?** – Explains the app’s functionality.
    *   **The App** – Details the application itself.
    *   **The Method** – Outlines the budgeting philosophy.
    *   **Why YNAB Is Different** – Highlights YNAB’s unique approach to budgeting.
    *   **Our 34-Day Trial** – Provides a pathway to start using the app.
    *   **The Ultimate Get Started Guide** – Offers a comprehensive guide for new users.
    *   **Free Workshops** – Provides access to educational workshops.
    *   **Blog** – Offers articles and insights.
    *   **Guides** – Provides detailed guides on various topics.
    *   **Help Center** – Offers support and assistance.

## Referral Program and Sharing

*   **Refer A Friend:** Users can earn rewards by referring friends to YNAB.
*   **Gift a YNAB Subscription:** Users can gift subscriptions to others.
*   **YNAB Together:** Allows users to subscribe together.
*   **Refer Your Employer:** Enables users to offer YNAB to their workplaces.
*   **Affiliate Program:** Provides opportunities for individuals to earn commissions by promoting YNAB.

## Pricing and Support

*   **Pricing:** Displays YNAB’s subscription pricing.

## Additional Information

The website also includes information about the pronunciation of “YNAB” and provides links to the YNAB blog, guides, and help center. It emphasizes the importance of sharing YNAB with others through the referral program and gifting options.
