# Toggl Track: Time Tracking Software

**Overview:** Toggl Track is time tracking software designed for teams and individuals to accurately track their time across various projects and workflows. It offers both free and paid plans to suit different needs.

**Key Features:**

*   **Time Tracking:** Easily start and stop timers to record time spent on tasks.
*   **Reporting:** Generate custom reports from time data to analyze productivity and profitability.
*   **Client Reports:** Create beautiful client reports to demonstrate time spent on projects.
*   **Integration:** Connect with over 100 online tools, including Slack and Jira.
*   **Multiple Plans:** Offers free and paid plans with varying features and limits.
