> [wakatime.com > Dashboards for developers](https://wakatime.com/)

WakaTime is a dashboard for developers, offering open-source IDE plugins. It provides tools for tracking development time, visualizing productivity, and managing projects. The platform focuses on providing insights into coding habits and helping developers optimize their workflow. Key features include time tracking, project management, and productivity analytics. The site emphasizes open-source development and community collaboration. It's designed to integrate seamlessly with popular IDEs. The site includes a logo and a navigation menu. The primary goal is to empower developers with data-driven insights into their coding activities. The content is geared towards developers seeking to improve their productivity and understand their development patterns.
