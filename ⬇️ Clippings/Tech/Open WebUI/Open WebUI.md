> [openwebui.com > Open WebUI](https://openwebui.com/)

Open WebUI is an extensible, self-hosted AI interface that adapts to your workflow, all while operating entirely offline. It supports LLM runners like Ollama and OpenAI-compatible APIs. Key features include new functions, a scheduler, and a task router. The site offers a signup process and a community forum. Enterprise plans are available upon request.
