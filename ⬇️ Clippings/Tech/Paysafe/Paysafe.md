# Paysafe Overview

Paysafe is a global payments technology company offering a comprehensive suite of payment solutions for businesses of all sizes. They cater to a wide range of industries and payment needs, including eCommerce, iGaming, and digital wallets.

## Key Payment Solutions

Paysafe provides various payment solutions, including:

*   **Online Payments:** Flexible and seamless online payments.
*   **Cash Online:** Online cash payments for new customers.
*   **Wallets:** Accept payments from digital wallets.
*   **Crypto Solutions:** Crypto payments simplified.
*   **eCommerce Solutions:** Intuitive checkout experiences.
*   **Pay by Bank:** Instant, secure transactions for iGaming.
*   **ISV & Platform Solutions:** Integrate payments into your software.
*   **Local Payment Methods:** Meet consumers' needs per market.
*   **Payment Acceptance Optimization:** Increase revenue and minimize declines.
*   **Affiliate Solutions:** Grow your business with affiliate marketing.
