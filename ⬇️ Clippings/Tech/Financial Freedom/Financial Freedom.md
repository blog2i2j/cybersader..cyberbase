# Financial Freedom - Open Source Financial Tracking

Financial Freedom is an open-source alternative to popular budgeting apps like Mint and YNAB. It empowers users to manage their finances and build wealth while maintaining control over their data and privacy. The project provides a comprehensive solution for tracking income, expenses, and investments.

## Key Features

*   **Budgeting:** Create and manage budgets based on your financial goals.
*   **Expense Tracking:** Automatically categorize and track your spending.
*   **Investment Tracking:** Monitor your investment portfolio performance.
*   **Open Source:** Benefit from community-driven development and transparency.
*   **Privacy Focused:** Maintain control over your financial data.
