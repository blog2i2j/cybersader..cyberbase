## Operation Privacy Overview

Operation Privacy is a DIY dashboard designed to empower users to manage their online presence, delete their information, and track their progress. The platform offers a free, no-third-party solution to combat stalking, doxxing, and swatting.

## Key Features

*   **Online Presence Management:** Tools to control and delete your information across various online platforms.
*   **Data Deletion:** Facilitates the removal of your data from websites and services.
*   **Progress Tracking:** Allows users to monitor their efforts to protect their privacy.
