# Redact - Mass Data Deletion Platform

Redact is a platform designed for the automated mass deletion of user data across 30+ online services, including Twitter, Reddit, Facebook, Discord, Steam, and more. The platform offers a free download and is available for Windows, macOS, and Linux.

## Key Features

*   **Multi-Service Support:** Deletion capabilities across a wide range of platforms.
*   **Scheduled Deletion:** Users can schedule data deletion intervals (3 months, 6 months, 1 year, or custom).
*   **Smart Deletion:**  Deletion based on keywords, sentiment, content type, and other criteria.
*   **Data Types:**  Deletion of posts, DMs, photos, videos, likes, and other content types.
*   **User Interface:**  A user-friendly interface for managing deletion processes.
