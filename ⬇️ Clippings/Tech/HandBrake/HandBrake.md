# HandBrake: Open Source Video Transcoder

HandBrake is a free and open-source, GPL-licensed video transcoder, available on Windows, Mac, and Linux. It converts video files from nearly any format to modern, widely supported codecs.

## Key Features

*   Supports conversion from nearly any video format.
*   Free and Open Source.
*   Multi-Platform Compatibility (Windows, Mac, Linux).
*   Multi-threaded processing for faster conversion.
