## Todoist Overview

Todoist is a task management and to-do list application designed to increase productivity, focus, and organization. It offers solutions for activity management, project management, time management, and teamwork.

## Features & Solutions

*   **Activity Management:** Manage daily tasks and activities.
*   **Project Management:** Organize and track projects with multiple tasks and subtasks.
*   **Time Management:** Schedule tasks and set reminders to optimize your time.
*   **Teamwork:** Collaborate with team members on shared projects and tasks.

## Resources

*   **Integrations:** Connect Todoist with other apps and services.
*   **Templates:** Utilize pre-built templates for various projects and tasks.

*   **Service Center:** Access support and resources.
