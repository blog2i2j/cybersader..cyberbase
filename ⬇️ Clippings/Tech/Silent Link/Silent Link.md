# Silent Link Overview

Silent Link offers borderless connectivity in 160+ countries with a pay-as-you-go model, eliminating KYC requirements, data limits, and expirations. Users can pay with Bitcoin, Lightning, Monero, USDT, or other cryptocurrencies.

## Key Features

*   **Global Coverage:** Operates in 160+ countries.
*   **No KYC:** No Know Your Customer verification.
*   **Unlimited Data:** No data limits or throttling.
*   **No Expiration:** Accounts and funds never expire.
*   **Payment Options:** Supports Bitcoin, Lightning, Monero, USDT, and other cryptocurrencies.
*   **Low Rates:** Data rates as low as $1.50/GB in Europe and the USA.
*   **Hotspot Enabled:** Hotspot functionality is available on all plans.

## Pricing and Plans

*   **Buy:** Purchase Silent Link access.
*   **Top up:** Add funds to your account.
*   **Renew:** Extend your subscription.
*   **Pricing:** Check pricing and service areas [https://silent.link/rates](https://silent.link/rates).
*   **Plans:** Offers various plans, including PLUS and IDENTITY/NO NUMBER, with different providers and migration limitations.

## Payment Methods

Users can pay with USDT or altcoins via the 4th Altcoins button.  The website also supports Bitcoin, Lightning, and Monero.

## Contact & Updates

Silent Link announces issue resolutions on Twitter [https://twitter.com/silentlink1](https://twitter.com/silentlink1) and provides a FAQ section.
