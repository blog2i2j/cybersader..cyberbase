# About GlintPay

GlintPay is a platform offering a digital gold buying, saving, and spending experience. It allows users to purchase physical gold, securely store it, and utilize it through a Glint Mastercard®.

## Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a reliable form of money, empowering clients with greater control and monetary freedom. We aim to offer an alternative to traditional fiat currencies.

## Key Features

*   **Digital Gold:** Buy gold bullion digitally.
*   **Flexibility:** Save and spend allocated gold with the flexibility of a Mastercard®.
*   **Secure Storage:** Securely store physical gold.

## Our Vision

We envision a world where everyone has an equal opportunity to prosper, fostering financial fairness for all.

## Investors

GlintPay is backed by a group of world-class investors, including British Business Bank, TOCOM, Sprott, and Sibanye.
