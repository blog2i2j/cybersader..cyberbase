# OpenBB Company Overview

OpenBB is a modern investment research terminal built on open source principles, driven by a community of developers. The company was founded in 2021 and focuses on providing scalable financial analysis solutions. Key aspects of OpenBB include:

*   **AI and Open Source:** Leveraging artificial intelligence and open-source technology to disrupt the financial industry.
*   **Team and Vision:** Featuring a dedicated team and a commitment to empowering the future of finance through open-source solutions.
*   **Workspace Product:** OpenBB Workspace is a product that allows users to bring any type of data into the product (structured, unstructured, API, databases, notes).
*   **Key Milestones:**
    *   **Dec 2023:** First demo of OpenBB Workspace in San Francisco.
    *   **May 2024:** OpenBB Workspace unveiled at Tower Research Ventures, introducing OpenBB Copilot.
    *   **Oct 2024:** OpenBB Workspace generally available with a free tier.
    *   **Mar 2025:** OpenBB Workspace becomes enterprise-ready with features like VPC, on-prem deployment, and SOC 2 Type II compliance.
*   **Team and Growth:**
    *   100K+ Registered Users
    *   37K+ Github Stars
    *   250+ Firms Using OpenBB
*   **Leadership Team:** Didier Lopes

# OpenBB Workspace

OpenBB Workspace is a product that allows users to bring any type of data into the product (structured, unstructured, API, databases, notes).

# OpenBB Copilot

OpenBB Copilot was introduced at Tower Research Ventures, where the team highlighted how AI agents could consume data available in the workspace.
