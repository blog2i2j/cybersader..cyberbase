> [ueli.app > Ueli - A Cross-Platform Keystroke Launcher](https://ueli.app/)

Ueli is a cross-platform keystroke launcher. It allows users to quickly launch applications and search by typing keystrokes. The application is developed by Oliver Schwendener and is available on Windows, macOS, and Linux. It features a dark logo and provides a way to streamline workflow through keyboard shortcuts. The application includes a development branch and a wiki for further information. It is supported through sponsorship.
