**Khoj AI: Your Open, Personal AI**

Khoj AI is an open, personal AI designed to answer questions from your documents or the web. It allows you to build custom agents, schedule automations, and chat in your native language. It transforms any LLM into your research copilot.

**Key Features:**

*   **Personal AI:** Tailored recommendations from specialist AI agents.
*   **Custom Agents:** Build and manage your own AI agents.
*   **Automations:** Schedule automated tasks.
*   **Multimodal Capabilities:** Answer complex questions across documents and the web, visualize concepts, and utilize voice chat.
*   **Autonomous Operations:** Delegate repetitive tasks, receive research and event-based notifications, and create custom newsletters.

**Core Functionality:**

*   **Research Copilot:** Turn any LLM into your research copilot.
*   **Document Understanding:** Answer complex questions woven across your documents and the web.
*   **Content Generation:** Generate images, diagrams, and charts.
*   **Voice Interaction:** Engage in voice chat.

**Supported Use Cases:**

*   **Technical Lead:** Simplify complex technical research.
*   **Copywriter:** Generate creative content.
*   **Custom Agent:** Build a specialized AI agent for your specific needs.
*   **Automated Research:** Receive daily summaries of AI tech startups and dynamic event-based notifications.

**Technology:**

*   **Multimodal:** Integrates text, voice, and visual data.
*   **LLM Powered:** Leverages large language models for advanced understanding and generation.
