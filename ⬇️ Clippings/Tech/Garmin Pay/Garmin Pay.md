# Garmin Pay™ | A Contactless Payment Solution

This page details Garmin Pay™, a contactless payment solution integrated into Garmin devices. It highlights key product categories and features.

## Products

Garmin Pay™ is primarily offered through a range of Garmin smartwatches, categorized as follows:

*   **Smartwatches:** This includes various smartwatch models designed for diverse activities and user preferences.
    *   **Fashion & Hybrid Smartwatches:** Featuring stylish designs with smartwatch functionality.
    *   **MARQ Luxury Watch Collection:** Premium smartwatches with advanced features.
    *   **Running:** GPS running smartwatches with advanced training metrics.
    *   **Multisport & Triathlete:** Smartwatches designed for multi-sport athletes.
    *   **Adventure:** Rugged smartwatches for outdoor adventures.
    *   **Swimming:** Smartwatches with swim tracking and dive computer features.
    *   **Diving:** Dive computers for underwater exploration.
    *   **Golf:** Smartwatches with golf course mapping and GPS.
    *   **Fitness & Health Tracking:** Track activity and health metrics.
    *   **Kids Smartwatch:** Smartwatches designed for children.

*   **Maps:** Outdoor maps for navigation.
    *   **Golf Course Locator:** Find nearby golf courses.

*   **Accessories & Plans:**
    *   **Accessories:** A range of accessories for Garmin devices.
    *   **Apps:** Downloadable apps for enhanced functionality.
    *   **Subscription Plans:** Various subscription options.
