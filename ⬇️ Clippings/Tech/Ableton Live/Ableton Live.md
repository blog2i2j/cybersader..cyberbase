# Ableton Live 12 - New Features

Ableton Live 12 introduces a suite of new features, devices, sounds, and workflow updates. Key highlights include:

- **New Devices:** Several new devices are available, expanding creative possibilities.
- **New Sounds:** A wide range of new sounds are included, offering diverse sonic palettes.
- **Workflow Updates:** Improvements to the user interface and workflow enhance productivity and ease of use.
