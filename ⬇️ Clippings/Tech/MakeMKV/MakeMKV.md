# MakeMKV Overview

MakeMKV is a software application designed to convert Blu-ray and DVD discs into MKV files. It offers a one-click solution for this process, preserving the original video and audio content while removing encryption.

## Key Features

*   **Disc Reading:** Reads both DVD and Blu-ray discs.
*   **Encryption Support:** Reads Blu-ray discs protected by the latest versions of AACS and BD+.
*   **Format Preservation:** Preserves all video and audio tracks, including HD audio.
*   **Metadata Retention:** Preserves chapter information and all meta-information (track language, audio type).
*   **Fast Conversion:** Converts as quickly as the drive allows.
*   **No Additional Software:** Requires no additional software for conversion or decryption.
*   **Cross-Platform Compatibility:** Available for Windows, macOS, and Linux.
*   **Free Functionality:** The DVD reading functionality is free and will remain free.
*   **Beta Features:** All features (including Blu-ray decryption and processing) are free during the BETA period.
