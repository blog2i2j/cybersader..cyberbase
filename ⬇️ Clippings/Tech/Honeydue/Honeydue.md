# Honeydue: How It Works

## Overview
Honeydue is a free mobile app designed to help couples collaboratively manage their finances. It simplifies tracking accounts, coordinating bills, and communicating about money.

## Key Features

*   **Collaborative Account Tracking:** Connect and monitor all your financial accounts – bank accounts, loans, investments – from over 20,000 institutions across 5 countries.
*   **Bill Coordination:** Receive bill reminders to avoid late fees and ensure timely payments.
*   **Communication:**  Built-in chat functionality allows for easy discussion and clarification of financial transactions.
*   **Security:**  Utilizes industry-standard best practices to protect your financial data and identity.

## Mission
Honeydue’s mission is to empower couples to ‘outsmart money, together.’

## Target Audience

Honeydue caters to couples of all ages and financial situations, from newly established relationships to long-term partnerships.

## Core Value Proposition

It takes a team to tackle money. Honeydue provides a streamlined and collaborative approach to financial management, fostering transparency and shared responsibility within a couple’s finances.

## Additional Information

*   Free to use.
*   Available on iOS and Android.
*   Focuses on security and data protection.
