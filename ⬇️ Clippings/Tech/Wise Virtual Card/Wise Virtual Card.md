# Wise Virtual Card Overview

The Wise Virtual Card is a digital debit card designed for safe online, in-store, and international spending. It's available to both personal and business customers. The card allows users to spend in multiple currencies while avoiding traditional currency conversion fees.

## Key Features

*   **Multi-Currency Account:** Manage funds in various currencies.
*   **Send Money:** Easily send money to others.
*   **Send Large Amounts:** Facilitates sending substantial sums.
*   **Receive Money:** Receive payments from around the world.
*   **Interest:** Earn interest on your balances.

## Card Benefits

*   **Secure Spending:** Provides an extra layer of security for online transactions.
*   **No Conversion Fees:** Avoids standard currency conversion charges.
*   **Global Access:** Spend in multiple currencies worldwide.
