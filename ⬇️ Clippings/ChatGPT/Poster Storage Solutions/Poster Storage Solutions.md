| --- | --- | --- | --- |

## Quick install notes

1. **Hit a stud or use 50 lb anchors** for screw models—acrylic can flex if the back isn’t flush.
2. On adhesive ledges, let the strips cure 60 min before loading; full strength at 24 h.
3. **UV & dust:** loaders block ~60 % UVA; pairing with an acrylic lip adds another ~10 %, but keep prized autos out of direct sun or rotate quarterly.
4. Clean with microfiber + plain water; avoid ammonia—it clouds both PVC sleeves and acrylic.

## 5 | How to Plan Your Layout

1. **Mark level lines** with painter’s tape first; once VHB touches paint, repositioning is hard.
2. **Weight check:** a sleeved 8 × 10 weighs ~6 oz—any strip rated 2 lb/foot is overkill.
3. **Gap choice:** leave at least ¼ in vertical above each rail so you can lift the loader straight out.
4. **Cleaning & UV:** wipe rails with plain water + microfiber; both PVC and J-channel plastics block ~60 % UVA, but avoid direct sunlight for signed pieces.
