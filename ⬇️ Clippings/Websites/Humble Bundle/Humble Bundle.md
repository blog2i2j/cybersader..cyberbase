# Humble Bundle Overview

This page is the homepage for Humble Bundle, an online retailer specializing in game bundles, book bundles, and software bundles. The core mission of Humble Bundle is to provide customers with access to a wide range of digital content while simultaneously supporting charitable organizations.  The site operates on a ‘pay what you want’ model, allowing customers to contribute any amount they choose, with a portion of their payment going directly to selected charities.

# Key Features and Information

*   **Bundle Sales:** Humble Bundle offers curated bundles containing multiple games, books, or software titles at discounted prices.
*   **Charitable Giving:** A significant portion of all purchases is donated to charities, with options to select specific charities to support.
*   **‘Pay What You Want’ Model:** Customers determine the price they pay, fostering a community-driven approach to supporting both content creators and charitable causes.
*   **Website Established:** Humble Bundle has been operational since 2010.
*   **Donations:** To date, Humble Bundle customers have donated over $267,000,000 to charity.
