# Plain Text Accounting (PTA) Overview

Plain Text Accounting (PTA) is a bookkeeping system utilizing plain text files and scriptable command-line software, such as Ledger, hledger, or Beancount. The site serves as a central repository for PTA community tools, documentation, and practices, maintained by Simon Michael and contributors.
