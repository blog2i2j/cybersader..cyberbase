# Privacy Guides: An Overview

Privacy Guides is a non-profit resource established in 2021 dedicated to providing independent privacy and security tools and information. It aims to help users protect their digital lives.

## Key Features

*   **Resource Directory:** Offers a collection of privacy tools.
*   **Educational Content:** Provides information on protecting online data privacy and security.
