> [techlore.tech > Digital Rights for All](https://www.techlore.tech/)

Techlore is a digital rights education platform dedicated to informing and empowering individuals regarding digital rights, privacy, security, and digital control. The site offers resources including a blog, video content, a forum, and coaching services. Key features include the ‘Go in Cognito’ initiative and a support system. The site's primary goal is to promote a safer internet by raising awareness and providing tools for individuals to protect their digital rights. The site is structured with a blog, video content, a forum, coaching, resources, and support.
